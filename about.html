Our Story

BUILT FOR EVERYONE

JustGym was founded on one belief: premium fitness should be accessible to all, not just the few.

Founded 2019

WHY WE EXIST

JustGym started when founder Alex Morgan walked out of a £200/month gym in frustration — not at the price, but at the attitude. Exclusive. Intimidating. Built for the already-fit.

We built JustGym for the person who's never stepped foot in a gym before, and for the athlete who trains six days a week. Same equipment. Same coaches. Same welcome.

Today we have over 2,400 members across London, and every single one is treated exactly the same on day one as they are on day one thousand.
