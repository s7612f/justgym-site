FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS

Is there a joining fee?

No joining fee, ever. Pay your first month and you're in. That's it.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel online or at reception with 30 days' notice. No questions, no penalties.

What's included in the free trial?

7 full days of access to everything in your chosen plan — no card required to start. You'll only be billed after the trial if you choose to continue.

Can I upgrade or downgrade?

Any time. Changes take effect the following billing cycle. Upgrade instantly — downgrade with 30 days' notice.

Do you offer student or corporate discounts?

Yes — 15% off for students with a valid ID. Corporate accounts available for teams of 5+. Contact us for details.

Can I freeze my membership?

Elite and Pro members can freeze for up to 3 months per year with no charge. Useful for holidays or injury recovery.
